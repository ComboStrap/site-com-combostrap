====== ComboStrap Styling -  Typography ======

===== About =====
This page regroups all [[styling:typo|typographic]] component of ComboStrap



===== Attributes =====

''Typographic Attribute'' are [[styling|styling attribute]]
  * [[text-align]]
  * [[font-size]]
  * [[text-color]]
  * [[boldness]]


===== Component =====
The below components are specific typographic components:

Heading:
  * [[ui:heading|heading]] 
      * structural heading (table of content)
      * typographical heading (component heading)

Text:
  * [[ui:text|text]] : apply [[#attributes|typographics attributes]] to one or several paragraph at once
  * [[ui:itext|itext]] : apply [[#attributes|typographics attributes]] to one or several characters/words at once






===== Typographics markup =====

==== Bold ====

To modify the [[styling:boldness|boldness]] (known as font-weight), you can use ''double star'', ''name'', ''value'' or ''superlatif''. Check the dedicated page: [[styling:boldness]]

Example with the [[ui:itext|itext component]]
<webcode name="Default" frameborder="0">
  * Input
<code dw>
This text is a <itext boldness="bold">bold text</itext> and this one **too**.
</code>
  * Output:
</webcode>
