====== ComboStrap Styling -  Typographic Attributes ======

===== About =====
''Typographic Attribute'' are [[styling|styling attribute]] that may be applied on:
    * ''paragraph'' with the [[ui:text|text component]]
    * ''words'' with the [[ui:itext|itext component]]
