====== ComboStrap Styling - Design System ======


===== About =====
The ''ComboStrap design'' system takes all the good part of [[docs:utility:bootstrap|Boostrap]] but does not stop there as it also integrates [[#material design|Google Material Design recommandations]]


===== Material Design =====

''ComboStrap'' applies the following [[https://material.io/|Google Material Design]] on:
  * [[skin|skin]]: to set the level of emphasis on button.
  * [[shadow|elevation]]: to a elevate a a single component and give more emphasis
  * [[docs:list:content-list|list]]: powerful list with visual, text and meta information
and integrates also the [[docs:content:icon|material design icons]]
