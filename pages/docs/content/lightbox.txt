====== Lightbox ======



===== About =====
A ''lightbox'' is a pop-up window that shows an [[docs:content:image|image]] by filling the screen.


===== Example =====

If you click on this [[docs:content:image|image]] a pop-up will appear, showing the image in full  mode (ie its natural size up to the screen size)

{{:docs:block:stock_image_surfer_in_the_see.png?200|}}

To close the pop-up, you can click:
  * on the close button located at the top right corner
  * or on the dark background outside the image.
