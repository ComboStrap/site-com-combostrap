====== The application Language ======


===== About =====
You can set the [[:docs:locale:lang|language]] for your whole [[app|application]] with the [[doku>config:lang]] [[docs:app:configuration|configuration]]. 

{{:docs:app:interface_language.jpg|}}

This is the default language for all text of your application:
  * for the language page
  * for the [[:docs:styling:lang|language component]]
