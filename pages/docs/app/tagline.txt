====== ComboStrap App - Tagline ======


===== About =====
A tagline is a short and appealing marketing description for your website.

It's used:
  * in the ''description'' field of the [[docs:app:manifest|manifest]]
  * in the [[docs:page_type:home#root|root home page]] [[docs:page_meta:title|title]].
  * in the home page [[..:page_type:website|website metadata]] as site description.




===== Enable =====

To enable it, you just need to set the [[doku>config:tagline|tagline]] [[:configuration|configuration]] with a value.
