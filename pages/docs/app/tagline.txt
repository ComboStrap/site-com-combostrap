====== ComboStrap App - Tagline / Slogan ======


===== About =====
A tagline is a short and appealing marketing description for your website.

It's used:
  * in the ''description'' field of the [[docs:app:manifest|manifest]]
  * in the [[docs:type:home#root|root home page]] [[docs:page:title|title]].
  * in the home page [[..:type:website|website metadata]] as site description.
  * in a [[docs:type:organization|organisation page]]




===== Enable =====

To enable it, you just need to set the [[doku>config:tagline|tagline]] [[docs:app:configuration|configuration]] with a value.
