===== About =====
''branding'' is the process of creating a coherent design that is linked to your company.

Through color palette and graphic design, your are passing a message of meaning, emotion and symbolism of what your company is.


ComboStrap supports the following ''branding'' component:
  * your [[logo|logo]]
  * your [[color|primary and secondary colors]] and its inheritance feature.
