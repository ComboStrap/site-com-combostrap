====== Search Engine Result Pages (SERP) ======

===== About =====
[[kb>serp|Search Engine Result Pages (SERP)]] are the pages that are shown when you use a [[seo|search engine]] such as Google.


If your pages show up, the users will see:
  * the [[docs:page:title|page title]]
  * the [[docs:page:url|page url]]
  * the [[docs:page:description|description]] (([[https://developers.google.com/search/docs/beginner/seo-starter-guide#use-the-description-meta-tag|Google may generate its own description]]))


The search engine may also add extra information that are created with the [[docs:type:type|type of your page]].
