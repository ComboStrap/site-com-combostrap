====== ComboStrap UI - Brand ======

===== About =====
''brand'' is a [[docs:component|component]] that goes generally in the left side of the [[docs:menubar:menubar|menubar]]. 

It highlights and helps styling the ''brand'' depicted as text and/or [[docs:content:icon|icon]] and adds a [[docs:content:link|link]] to your home page.
