====== Main Side Slot ======


===== About =====
The ''main side slot'' is a [[slot|slot]] that is located at the right side of the [[docs:layout:page:main|main slot]].

===== How it works =====
This is a standard [[docs:layout:page:slot|slot feature]]

  * Create a page called ''slot_main_side'' with the help of the [[docs:layout:page:slot_manager|slot manager]]
  * And you will see its content appears at the right side of the main content.
