====== Landing Page ======



===== About =====
A ''landing page'' is a page with big horizontal sections that shows off your product or services.


===== How to create a landing page ? =====

Within Combostrap, you can create a landing page by adding one or more [[:docs:layout:component:bar|bar component]] in your [[:docs:layout:page:main|main slot]].


Each bar will then create a section of page where you may add:
  * separator 
  * or [[docs:styling:background|background color]] to distinguish them




Example:
<code dw>
<bar size="lg">
    <background color="light"/>
    Section 1
</bar>
<bar size="lg">
    <background color="blue"/>
    Section 2
</bar>
</code>
