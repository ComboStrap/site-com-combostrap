====== ComboStrap UI - Breadcrumb ======

===== About =====
A ''breadcrumb'' is a [[docs:navigation:navigation|navigational component]] that shows a list of pages.



===== Type =====

We supports two type of ''breadcrumb''.

  * The classic [[docs:navigation:hierarchical_breadcrumb|Hierarchical breadcrumb]] that helps the reader to find their place within a website and shows where the page is located in the category structure.
  * and a [[docs:navigation:historical_breadcrumb|Historical breadcrumb]] that shows the navigation history (the last pages that were visited)
