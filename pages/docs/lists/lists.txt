====== ComboStrap - Lists ======

===== About =====
ComboStrap supports the following ''list'' [[:docs:component|components]]
  * [[:docs:lists:list|List]] - simple ordered or unordered list, 
  * [[docs:lists:content-list|Content-list]] - continuous, vertical indexes of text or images. 
  * [[docs:lists:table|Table]] - a list of records
