====== ComboStrap - Permanent Redirect ======

===== About =====
An ''permanent redirect'' is a [[redirection|redirection method]] used by the [[url|URL manager]] that redirects [[#permanently|permanently]] a page to another page .


They are executed when:
  * when the [[docs:metadata:alias#redirect|alias type is redirect]].
  * if the [[page_rules|page rules redirection]] leads to an existent page.


===== Permanently =====

Permanently means that the search engines will be instructed to:
  * delete the redirected page from their search index.
  * and update with the new one
In Web term, we perform a ''301'' redirection.
