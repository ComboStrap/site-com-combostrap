====== ComboStrap Quality - Automatic Hidden Artifactory ======


===== About =====

The [[:combo|combo plugin]] automatically [[quality:hidden|hides]]:
  * the [[strap:bar|bars]]
  * the ''combostrap'' private namespace (where [[ui:icon|icons]] and other internal component are saved)


This page resource are still accessible but should not be advertised to the outside world because they are just component and have [[quality:low_quality_page|low value]].

This is a component of the [[quality:quality|quality seo module]].
