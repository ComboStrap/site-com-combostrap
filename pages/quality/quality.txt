====== ComboStrap - Quality Module ======

===== About =====
The ''quality module'' aims to measure and keep the quality of the website high.

This module regroups the following functionalities:
  * [[quality:automatic_hidden|Automatic Hidden Artifactory]]
  * [[quality:lqpp|Low Quality Page Protection]]
  * [[rule|Quality rule]]
  * [[score|Quality score]]

<note>
The [[url:404|missing page management]] also improves the quality of the website.
</note>


===== Analytics =====

All the quality data can be found and queried under the ''quality'' node of the [[analytics:data|analytical data]].
