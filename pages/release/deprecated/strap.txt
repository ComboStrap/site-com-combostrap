====== Strap Template has been deprecated ======

===== About =====
''strap'' was the template of [[:combostrap|ComboStrap]] and is no more used since the version [[release:1.25|Release 1.25]].

  * It has been enhanced by the [[:docs:theme|theme feature]]. 
  * It's no more needed to install the [[doku>template:strap|strap template]] to get all template functionalities.
