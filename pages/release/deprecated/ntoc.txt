====== ComboStrap UI - Ntoc ======


===== About =====
The ''ntoc'' component is no more and has been renamed to [[ui:page-explorer|page-explorer]] where:
  * The ''index'' has become the ''home''
  * The ''page-item'' has become the ''page''
  * The ''ns-item'' has become the ''namespace''
