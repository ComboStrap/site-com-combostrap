====== Strap Template - Headerbar ======


===== About =====
The [[strap|strap template]] supports the creation of a [[bar|bar (page)]] called  ''headerbar'' that will be rendered at the top of the template.


The ''headerbar'' is supposed to contain a ''navbar''. See [[getting_started:5_headerbor|Creation of a headerbar]].
