====== ComboStrap UI - Container ======

===== About =====
A ''container'':
  * positions the content in the center
  * and limits the horizontal width (adjusted by [[styling:breakpoint|screen sizes]])



It's used in [[ui:layout|layout component]] such as:
  * [[strap:header_slot|page header]]
  * [[strap:footer_slot|page footer]]
  * and [[ui:slide|slide]]

<note>
The [[strap:layout|page layout]] ''holy'' and ''median'' have their main content by default contained.
</note>

===== Syntax and example =====


A container with a [[styling:color|light background color]] and a little bit of [[styling:spacing|padding]]

<code dw>
<container background-color="light" class="p-3">
Oh my Container !
</container>
</code>

\\
\\



<note important>
A container has an automatic margin that centers it. You should then not set the margin, otherwise it will be located to the left
</note>
