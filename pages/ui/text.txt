====== ComboStrap UI - Text ======


===== About =====
''text'' is a [[styling:typo|typographic]] component that permits to style one or several paragraph at once.

<badge>Since [[release:release|version 1.4]]</badge>
===== Articles Related =====
{{backlinks>.}}

===== Syntax =====

<code dw>
<text type>
First paragraph

Second paragraph
</text>
</code>
where:
  * [[styling:type|type]] can take the values:
      * ''lead'': It makes a paragraph stand out by setting a bigger font-size and weight
      * ''h1 to h6'': when you want to match the font styling of a [[heading|heading]].
      * ''custom class'' that you have created in the [[styling:userstyle|userstyle stylesheet]].
  * [[#line-spacing]] sets the space between the lines of a paragraph.
  * [[styling:text-color|color]] can take all [[styling:color|color values]] and the [[#color|specific text colors]]:
      * ''body'': the color of the body
      * ''muted'' : light gray
      * ''black-50'' : 50 black
      * ''white-50'' : 50 white
  * or any [[styling:typo|typographic attributes]] (font size, font weight, ...)

==== Line-Spacing ====

Example:

Ut enim ad minim veniam, quis nostrud exercitation ullamco laboris nisi ut aliquip ex ea commodo consequat.
</text>
<text line-spacing="sm" width="40rem">

==== Text-Align ====
[[styling:text-align|text-align]] lets you define where the line of text will be located inside the paragraph. <inote>A paragraph is a [[ui:block|block]] and takes by default all the available width</inote>



Example:
<webcode name="Default" frameborder="0">
<code dw>
<text text-align="start">
This line of text is at the **start** of a paragraph (left for a left-to-right language)
</text>
<text text-align="center">
This line of text is **centered** inside the paragraph block.
</text>
<text text-align="end">
This line of text is at the **end** of a paragraph (right for a left-to-right language)
</text>
</code>
  * Output:
</webcode>

<note>
Text alignment can also be **screen conditional**.

For all information about this attribute, see the dedicated page: [[styling:text-align]]
</note>
