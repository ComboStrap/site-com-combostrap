====== ComboStrap UI - Inote (inline note) ======


===== About =====
The ''inote'' is a [[note|note]] that can be added along side a text (ie inline). It gives emphasis on an ancillary text that adds context to the primary text. 

As a replacement, you could also use:
  * a [[tooltip|tooltip]]
  * or a [[doku>wiki:syntax#footnotes|footnote]].
