====== Container Component ======


===== About =====
The ''container'' component has been deprecated for [[:docs:theme:element:container|container aware component]].



===== Old =====

==== Example ====


A container:
  * that centers its content from the medium breakpoint ''md''
  * with a [[docs:styling:color|light background color]] 
  * and a little bit of [[docs:styling:spacing|padding]]

<webcode name="Default" frameborder="0">
<code dw>
<container md background-color="light" spacing="p-3">
Oh my Container !
</container>
</code>
</webcode>


==== Syntax ====

<code dw>
<container type>
Content
</container>
</code>
where the type was the Container configuration value.
