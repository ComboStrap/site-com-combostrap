====== ComboStrap Styling - OnVisible ======


===== About =====

<badge>[[release:beta:beta|Beta status]]</badge>

''OnVisible'' is an [[docs:animation:animation|animation attribute]] that permits to set an animation to a [[docs:block:block|block component]] when they enter the screen and become visible.





===== Example =====

==== Animation 1 ====

<webcode name="OnView" frameborder="0">
<code dw>
<title  onView="fadeInLeft">A title with a fadeInLeft animation</title>
</code>
</webcode>
